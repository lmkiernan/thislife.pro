Privacy Policy

The more honest people can be with themselves on This Life, the more This Life can work—helping us each live with greater peace toward ourselves and reality.

We understand that spoken notes are often deeply personal. We'll protect this information with as much care—human and technical—as we can.

We will never sell, transfer, or use your data for any outside purpose. We will never feature a spoken note publicly (e.g., as a "One of the Day") without your explicit permission. Below is a technical overview of how we protect your data.

This Life: Technical Infrastructure & Data Privacy

Hosting & Security

All services run on Amazon Web Services (AWS)—the same secure cloud infrastructure trusted by hospitals, banks, and governments. Key protections include:

  • AES-256 encryption at rest
  • TLS 1.2+ encryption in transit
  • FIPS 140-2 validated cryptography
  • SOC 2 Type II / ISO 27001 compliance

Encryption

  • At Rest: Data is encrypted with AES-256.
  • In Transit: Data is protected via TLS with Perfect Forward Secrecy.
  • Encryption keys are managed through AWS KMS with strict access controls.

Access Management

  • Access is limited by role and only granted if necessary.
  • All admin access requires multi-factor authentication (MFA).
  • Every access event is logged and regularly reviewed.

Data Retention

  • Data is stored in U.S.-based AWS data centers.
  • We automatically delete obsolete data and securely erase upon request.
  • We retain data only as long as required by law or for product functionality—no longer.

Third Parties

  • We do not share or sell personal data.
  • We only use trusted infrastructure partners (e.g., AWS), under strict processing agreements.
  • No cross-border data transfers without explicit user consent.

User Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your data. Contact us at privacy@thislife.app. We'll verify and fulfill your request unless legal retention is required.

We engineer for trust.
Security and privacy are built into every layer of This Life—from our code to our policies to the choices we make about how this app is used.
